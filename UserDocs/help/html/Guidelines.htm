Guidelines

Move the mouse pointer very near the upper edge of the document area and hold it steady for about 1.5 seconds - the ruler will appear. You can click on the ruler to add/remove vertical guidelines.

You can also right-click to the ruler to precisely define the guideline type you want to insert:

  • Normal guideline - when clicked, a math box is created
  • Text guideline - when clicked, a text box is created
  • Text autowrap guideline - object do not stick to it, but forces line wrap when you type text
In addition, local automatic guidelines are displayed when your mouse pointer is vertically aligned with nerby math/text objects.
