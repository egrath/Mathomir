Working with keyboard selections

When typing, you can make selections by holding down the shift key and using right or left arrow key. Selected part of your eqaution/text will be displayed blue.

After you made the selection you can use following keys to modify the selection:

- Ctrl+X - to cut the selection
- Ctrl+C - to copy the slection
- Del, Backspace - to delete the selection
- R, G - to change selection color (red, green)
- U, S, O - to underline, strikeout, overline the selection
- B, I - to bold, italic the selection
- / or # or ` - to put fraction line under the selection
- (, [, { - to put parentheses around selection
- 0...9 - to raise the selection to power
- C - to put the 'case' line
- \ - to put selection into a function argument
- Enter - to open the context menu over the selection
