Entering indexes and exponents

To enter an exponent you can use two methods:

  • Alt+key - to quickly enter a simple exponent
  • the ^ or the " key - to enter more complex exponents

Likewist, to enter an index you can use two methods:

  • SpaceBar+key - to quickly enter a simple index
  • the underline '_' key - to enter more complex index

To easily enter complex indexes, containg math operators, you can check the Options->Keyboard->Use complex indexes option.
