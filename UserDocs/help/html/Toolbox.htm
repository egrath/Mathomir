The Toolbox

First two options (‘U’ and ‘M’) control fonts. Other options represent mathematical elements and hand-drawing tools.

Sub-toolboxes

You can open a sub-toolbox by hovering the mouse pointer above any toolbox item.

Toolbox Accelerators

Right-click at any toolbox or sub-toolbox item to open the accelerator table. From the table you can choose any CTRL+ or double-stroke accelerator. In addition, you can also define an Easycast string sequence accelerator.

Adding new items into the toolbox

  • Create a new item anywhere in the document area
  • Pick-up the item with your mouse and carry it to the toolbox or sub-toolbox.
  • Right-click at the toolbox or sub-toolbox to drop the item into it.
